About Us

Professional tools born from direct experience.

Our Mission

FastQRfree was born out of a daily necessity. As developers, we often searched for reliable tools to generate graphic elements for our projects, only to find services limited by mandatory registrations, hidden costs, or expiring codes.

We decided to make a professional-grade QR code generator available to everyone: 100% free, anonymous, and in high resolution.

Simplicity and Quality

No account needed. Generate your code, download it in high quality (2000px), and use it forever. Whether it is for a business card or a large advertising billboard, the result will always be sharp.

Independent Development

We are a small team dedicated to creating micro-tools for the web. Our goal is to remove technical obstacles for professionals and creatives, offering immediate utility without any fluff or aggressive marketing.

Our Project Standards

Every tool on FastQRfree is developed following strict principles to ensure maximum professional utility:

  • Total Accessibility: The service is 100% free, with no subscriptions or hidden fees for high-resolution features.
  • Native Privacy: Security is not an option. We do not store your data on our servers; generation happens directly in your browser.
  • Efficient Workflow: Each file is automatically renamed based on the URL, eliminating confusion when managing large catalogs.
  • Data Integrity: We use Level H error correction to ensure readability even on irregular or worn print surfaces.

Quick Questions & Answers

Duration and Limits:

The generated codes are static and permanent: they never expire and can be scanned an unlimited number of times without any restrictions.

Post-Print Modifications:

Since the information is embedded directly into the QR pattern, it is not possible to change the link once printed. A new code must be generated for each destination change.

Size and Readability:

For optimal scanning across all devices, we recommend not going below 2x2 cm when printing, maintaining the original high-density proportions.

Tracking and Data:

FastQRfree does not track scans. Your URLs remain private and are not saved in external databases, guaranteeing maximum professional confidentiality.
